Computer-Aided Design (CAD) at ODES

At ODES, we leverage Computer-Aided Design (CAD) to bring innovative, high-precision designs to life for the automotive and mechanical engineering industries.

Our CAD Expertise:

  • 3D Modeling & Solid Modeling – Develop highly accurate 3D representations.
  • Parametric & Feature-Based Design – Optimize and modify designs efficiently.
  • 2D Drafting & Technical Drawings – Generate precise engineering drawings.
  • Sheet Metal & Surface Modeling – Design complex sheet metal structures.
  • Assembly & Mechanism Design – Simulate component interactions.
  • Manufacturing & Tooling Design – Create production-ready designs.

Why Choose ODES for CAD?

  • Advanced CAD Software: We use industry-leading tools.
  • Efficiency & Precision: Automated processes ensure high-quality outputs.
  • Seamless Integration with CAE & CAM: CAD models integrate smoothly.
  • Tailored Solutions: We provide customized CAD solutions.

Computer-Aided Engineering (CAE) Simulations

At ODES, we harness the power of Computer-Aided Engineering (CAE) to drive innovation and efficiency in the automotive and mechanical engineering industries. Our advanced simulation techniques enable businesses to analyze, optimize, and validate designs before manufacturing, reducing costs and enhancing product performance.

Our CAD Expertise:

  • Finite Element Analysis (FEA) – Evaluates structural integrity.
  • Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) – Simulates airflow and heat transfer.
  • Thermal & Stress Analysis – Prevent failures and improve longevity.
  • Multibody Dynamics (MBD) – Analyzes motion and interactions.

Transform Your Ideas into Reality with ODES!
By utilizing industry-leading CAE software and cutting-edge methodologies, ODES helps businesses refine designs, increase efficiency, and develop innovative solutions with confidence. Whether you're optimizing vehicle aerodynamics, structural components, or mechanical systems, our precision-driven approach ensures reliability and performance at every stage of development.
